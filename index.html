✦ The Artist

Rooted in the Southwest

Sabina Kuss is a Durango, Colorado-based painter whose work is deeply rooted in the landscapes, animals, and light of the American Southwest. Working primarily in oil, she captures subjects with a warmth and authenticity that only comes from truly living among them.

Her most beloved subjects are the burros of the Four Corners region — animals that carry a profound history of partnership with people across the rugged San Juan Mountains. In each painting, Sabina brings out the personality and soulfulness of these remarkable creatures.

Burro Fest
Mancos, Colorado

Sabina is proud to be part of the beloved Mancos Burro Fest — an annual celebration near Durango where artists and burros come together in an unforgettable event. Artists are paired with live burros weeks in advance, giving the work real depth and connection. You'll find Sabina's paintings alongside these extraordinary animals on Grand Avenue.

Event dates to be announced — follow along or reach out to be notified!
